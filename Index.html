About Me

I'm a Composer, Producer, Artist from Germany with a wide range of interests. Music was all around me since I was a little child and after growing up in a recording studio I decided to study music at the ArtEZ Conservatorium in Enschede, Netherlands. For almost 20 years now I compose music for films and commercials, record and produce other artists, play live and teach music. I also noodle around with 3D Software like Blender, write code for my own audio-PlugIns or websites and at the moment I'm studying Physics at the University of Potsdam.
